# **App Name**: OZNIOR

## Core Features:

- Product Browsing: Browse available fragrances and view their details such as description, price, and scent notes.
- Product Search & Filtering: Search for products by name or filter by category, tags and refine your search based on different attributes to find desired product quickly.
- Shopping Cart: Add products to a shopping cart, view cart contents, and update quantities before proceeding to checkout.
- Secure Checkout: Securely enter shipping information and mock payment details to complete the purchase of items in the shopping cart.
- Admin Authentication: Authenticate the user via credentials in order to gain administrator access.
- Order Management: Ability for administrators to view and manage customer orders, update order statuses, and track payments.
- Product Information Tool: Utilize generative AI to provide potential buyers with insights or complementary product suggestions that consider multiple criteria from product data and current customer reviews. The tool can decide when these insights can boost customer confidence.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep navy blue (#071028), evokes luxury and sophistication.
- Background color: Soft, muted gray (#F6F5F4) to provide contrast and highlight product imagery, desaturated from the primary color.
- Accent color: Gold (#C9A15B), conveys a sense of opulence and refinement. Approximately 30 degrees 'left' of the primary, also desaturated.
- Headline font: 'Playfair Display' (serif) for elegant headlines, emphasizing the premium brand aesthetic.
- Body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a clean, modern reading experience in product descriptions and general content.
- Use minimalist, geometric icons in gold or white to complement the overall aesthetic. Focus on clarity and simplicity.
- Maintain generous negative space to enhance the perception of luxury and exclusivity. Follow a grid-based system for organized content.
